CT3Tweaks

A tweak tool for Crazy Taxi 3.

Download

Features

  • Automatically optimizes the game for your computer
  • Allows choosing crispy resolutions above 1280x960
  • Increases the FPS limit from 30 to a buttery-smooth 60
  • Allows changing your field of view to a skating montage
  • Backs up the exe for those extra vulnerable moments
  • God damn do those background screenshots look good

Requirements

  • Windows Vista or later
  • .NET Framework v4.5 or later
  • FairLight's v1.0 game executable
  • A toaster or something
